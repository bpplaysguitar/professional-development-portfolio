About

My name is Brian Palay. I'm a full-stack web developer with a particular fondness for front-end development and design. I graduated from the University of Minnesota with a B.A. in architecture, and I'm currently studying full-stack web development, also at the Univerity of Minnesota. My work over the years has involved many forms of digital design, including graphic design, product photography, and 3D modeling and rendering.

I've always loved creating things - from Lego as a kid, to songs in my 15 years as a professional musician. In web development, I see a new and exciting opportunity to put my passion for creating to work.
